About Travelogo

Travelogo is a modern travel planning application that helps users organize their trips efficiently. The app provides features like:

  • Interactive trip planning and itinerary management
  • Real-time collaboration with travel companions
  • Smart recommendations for destinations and activities
  • Budget tracking and expense management
  • Photo sharing and travel journal features

Built with React Native and Firebase, Travelogo offers a seamless cross-platform experience for travelers worldwide.
